\subsubsection{Exception Handling}

Here's the Taylor's version of \cd{try-catch}, a common construct for exception handling:
\begin{minted}{text}
GetAway [
    <STATEMENTS>
] Car [<EXCEPTION>] [
    <STATEMENTS>
]
\end{minted}
\bd{Example:}
\begin{minted}{text}
GetAway [
    a = 51 / "a";
] Car [TypeError] [
    SpeakNow["That is not allowed."];
]
\end{minted}
