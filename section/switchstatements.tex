\subsubsection{Switch Statements}

Below is the Taylor's version of switch statement. Every \cd{Dear-John} statements must have a \cd{Closure} part.
\begin{minted}{text}
Dear [<EXPRESSIONS>] [
   John [<VALUE>] [
       <STATEMENTS>
   ]
   John [<VALUE>] [
       <STATEMENTS>
   ]
   .
   .
   .
   Closure [
       <STATEMENTS>
   ]
]
\end{minted}
\bd{Example:}
\begin{minted}{text}
c = 420 % 2;
Dear [c] [
    John [0] [
        SpeakNow["420 is an even number."];
    ]
    John [1] [
        SpeakNow["420 is an odd number."];
    ]
    Closure [
        Breathe;
    ]
]
\end{minted}
Output:
\begin{minted}{text}
420 is an even number.
\end{minted}
