\subsubsection{Input/Output Statements}
Here's the overview of the syntax:
\begin{minted}{text}
<IDENT> = Store[];
SpeakNow[<IDENT>] -- prints the content of <IDENT>
\end{minted}
\bd{Example:}
\begin{minted}{text}
var = Store["Give some value: "];
SpeakNow[var * var];
\end{minted}
Output:
\begin{minted}{text}
    Give some value: 6
    36
\end{minted}
